Direction 2: Spacious Centered

Based on Beatsy's core goal of creating an exciting party atmosphere, the Spacious Centered approach best captures the nostalgic, confident, and playfully competitive vibe. The generous whitespace and centered focus keeps players engaged on the current moment, while the larger visual elements (album art, timer, scores) create excitement that's visible across the room when someone's phone is held up.

The other directions could work as alternative "themes" - Compact Card for experienced players who want faster gameplay, and Dashboard Grid for tournament-style competitive sessions. But for the MVP party game experience, Spacious Centered hits the emotional target.
